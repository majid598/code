RaveBull, an Ethereum protocol, facilitates undercollateralized on-chain credit facilities, allowing borrowers to customize parameters to their preferences. Lenders benefit from innovative interest rate and withdrawal mechanisms.

How RaveBull Operates

Borrower Enrollment

Borrowers submit an onboarding request to RaveBull and undergo a protocol KYC process to obtain authorization for market deployment.

Asset Deposit by Lenders

Lenders deposit assets as credit, receiving interest-bearing debt tokens in return. These tokens are transferrable, offering flexibility in their usage.

Market Configuration

Borrowers define market terms upon deployment: APR, maximum borrowing limit, reserve ratio, withdrawal cycle length, and more.

Lender Authorization

Borrowers choose which addresses can lend to their markets. Wildcat does not restrict the lender selection process, leaving it to the borrower's discretion.

Credit Utilization by Borrowers

Borrowers can access a portion of the extended credit, returning assets upon withdrawal requests from lenders.

The Rest Is Up to You

Borrowers can adjust APR and market capacity as needed, contingent upon existing debt and liquid asset amounts in market reserves.
